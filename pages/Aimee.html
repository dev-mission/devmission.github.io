Engineering Development Specialist at Uber

Aimee Gonzalez

  • Speciality: Engineering Development
  • Fun Fact:
  • San Francisco

Part of the Engineer Training and Development team, Aimee continue to apply learning theory and educational research to curriculum development and collaborate with engineering instructors to strengthen their teaching skills, with the longer-term goal of transforming the way adults learn at work.

Aimee also assists in growing and scaling course offerings to Uber engineers in the rest of the US, create and teach workshops for instructors, run the tech talk event series (which includes providing speaker support on slides and presentation delivery) and lead UX for online learning and knowledge resource access points.
