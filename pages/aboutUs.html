About Us

In early 2017, <dev/Mission> was founded by Leonardo Sosa and a team of technologists, that care about STEM opportunities for untapped young adults. Our goal is to connect the most overlooked population in San Francisco, Bay Area and beyond; and expose them to careers in the tech industry.
