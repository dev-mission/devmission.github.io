About Us

In early 2017, dev/Mission was founded by Leonardo Sosa and a team of technologists, that care about STEM opportunities for diverse young adults. Our goal is to connect the most overlooked population in San Francisco, Bay Area and beyond; and expose them to careers in the tech industry.

Leadership Team

Our values: Integrity, Community, Diversity, Equality, Responsibility and Motivation.

Tech Mentors Team

Our tech mentors serve as adult role models and provide support and guidance as the youth experience new challenges and unfamiliar social environments. At times they may need someone to help them navigate their career path, advice on education and training, or just a friend to talk to.

Web Development Team

Four young adults came together to build our dev/Mission website using their skills, puting their time, hard work, and dedication.

IT Department

Our information technology department, responsible for the architecture, hardware, software and networking of our computers.
